Life & Career

Jordyn Thoreson studied Advertising at the University of Florida and graduated in the spring of 2023. By August, she secured a full-time position as a Communications Assistant for the UF Human Resources (UFHR) Department of Communications and Worklife. Shortly after, Jordyn decided to pursue graduate studies at the University of Florida, where she is currently pursuing a Master of Arts in Mass Communication (MAMC) with a concentration in Web Design and Online Communication. She has also since been promoted to Communications Specialist in her role at UFHR.

Learn More

While Jordyn enjoys working with internal communications and programming, her ultimate career goals include B2C agency work and, eventually, freelancing and working with small businesses and non-profits to optimize their branding and online presence. Once she completes her graduate studies, Jordyn plans to pursue these goals and hopes to make a lasting impact on her community through her work.
